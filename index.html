We're hosting an online event to kick off our club. Anyone with an interest in making cool art with math or meeting some new friends is welcome!

What?

These images were made with math, and we'd love to show you how! We're hosting a generative art workshop, where we'll be showing you how to make your own math pictures using strange attractors and Processing. You are welcome to download Processing ahead of time, but we'll also show you how to use online development environments as well. If you'd just like to come for the games, we'll also have games running with two $20 grad club gift cards up for grabs for the winners!
